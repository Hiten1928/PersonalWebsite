Self-Adaptive Systems

My research was centered at enhancing the reliability and performance of self-adaptive systems as a whole. Self-Adaptive Systems are ubiquitous, ranging from this website you are watching to IBM Watson. My work focussed in increasing the resiliency of the system by making it pro-active about the future changes that the system should be prepared for.

I developed an algorithm that would include Markov's Decision Process and enhance its decision making process by incorporating factors necessari for safety critical systems such as Unmanned Aerial Vehicles(UAV's)

I am a Graduate Assistant with Prof. Daniel Krutz in Rochester Institute of Technology.


Microserives

Along with Self-Adaptive systems I have worked in the area of Microservices and focussed in the Testing of Microservices. My work was guided by the fact that in a complex microservices it is not possible to pin-point the location of the microservices which fail and it is a tedious task that falls on the developers domain. This problem can be solved in order to make microservices testing feasible and much more granular in nature.

I am working with Prof. Naveen Sharma, Department Chair(Software Engineering), RIT in this topic.

Software Evolution(Performance Regression)

My future plans are to apply the pro-active nature of self adaptive systems to the domain of Software Evolution and enhance the process of software maintaince by automating the task of reduction in Performance Regression
